The standard

Detailed guidance
The complete list
✓
Approved
Wear this
✓Two or three-piece suit — navy, charcoal, grey, or cream
✓Silk tie, knit tie, or formal bow tie
✓Pocket square — white linen is always safe
✓Leather Oxford, Derby, loafer, or monk-strap
✓Dress shirt — white, pale blue, or subtle stripe
✓Cufflinks if wearing a double-cuff shirt
✓Linen or wool blend for warm-weather comfort
✓Full-length dress socks — over-the-calf preferred
✕
Forbidden
Leave this at home
✕Chinos — in any colour, weight, or cut
✕Jeans — including dark wash or "smart" denim
✕Shorts of any kind
✕Sneakers, trainers, or casual footwear
✕Sports or outdoor fabrics
✕Cropped or ankle-length trousers — a full break required
✕No-show socks — full-length dress socks only
✕Blazer with casual trousers and no tie
✕Novelty socks with short-break trousers
