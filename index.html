Getting There

Gordes is proper countryside — a rental car is strongly recommended either way you fly in.

From Nice

✈ ~2h30 by car
  1. Fly into Nice Côte d'Azur (NCE)
  2. Drive: A8 west, then A7/A51 toward Avignon, exit via D900 & D2 into Gordes
  3. Or train + car: TGV to Avignon (~2h40), pick up a rental car, ~45 min drive to Gordes

From Marseille

✈ ~1h15 by car
  1. Fly into Marseille Provence (MRS)
  2. Drive: A7 north toward Avignon, exit at Cavaillon, follow D2 into Gordes
  3. Or train + car: TGV to Avignon (~35 min), pick up a rental car, ~45 min drive to Gordes
🚕
Taxis and rideshares are scarce out here. If you're not renting a car, let us know in the form below and we'll help arrange a shared transfer with other guests.
